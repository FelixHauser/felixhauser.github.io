Erasmus Tracker

Expense Management for Erasmus+ Mobility Projects

Free • No Ads • Privacy-First

Download on the App Store

Purpose

Erasmus Tracker is a comprehensive expense management tool designed specifically for teachers coordinating Erasmus+ mobility projects. It simplifies budget tracking across multiple funding categories, handles multi-currency expenses, generates professional PDF reports, and ensures accurate financial reporting to the European Commission. The app uses official Erasmus+ terminology and requires no account or external servers—all data stays on your device.

About This Project

I developed Erasmus Tracker after experiencing firsthand the complexity of managing Erasmus+ finances with spreadsheets and paper receipts. Coordinating mobility projects involves tracking four separate budget categories, managing expenses in multiple currencies, calculating refunds owed to the EU, and generating detailed reports—all while ensuring compliance with European Commission guidelines.

The app remains free and privacy-focused because it serves educators who are already investing significant time and effort into creating life-changing experiences for their students. No subscriptions, no data collection, no complexity—just straightforward expense tracking that respects your time and privacy while helping you stay organized and compliant.

Features

Four Budget Categories

Track Individual Support for Students and Teachers, Travel Grant, and Organizational Support separately. Uses official Erasmus+ terminology in English, German, Spanish, and Catalan.

Multi-Currency Support

Handle expenses in foreign currencies with automatic conversion. Track exchange rates and see amounts in both local and primary currencies.

Receipt Photos

Attach photos to expenses for documentation. All photos are stored locally or synced privately through your iCloud Drive.

Professional PDF Reports

Generate comprehensive PDF reports with budget breakdowns, expense tables, and receipt documentation. Perfect for submitting to National Agencies.

Real-Time Budget Monitoring

See at a glance how much you've spent and how much remains in each category. Automatic calculation of refunds owed to the EU.

iCloud Sync

Optionally sync your data across devices using your personal iCloud account. All data remains encrypted and private.

Face ID Protection

Secure sensitive financial data with Face ID. Authentication happens entirely on your device.

Privacy-First Design

No accounts. No external servers. No data collection. No analytics. Everything stays on your device unless you enable iCloud sync.

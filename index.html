About

I develop iOS applications as personal projects—tools that solve practical problems I've encountered in my own work and studies. Each app is built with a focus on functionality and simplicity, free from unnecessary complexity or commercial distractions.

My approach is straightforward: identify a need, build a clean solution, and share it freely. No subscriptions, no advertisements, no data collection. Just useful software that does what it's supposed to do.
